---
title: Page Not Showing Up
description: Fix missing or invisible documentation pages.
---

## Symptoms

- Page is missing from the sidebar
- Newly added content isn't visible

## Causes

- Page not published
- Cached browser content

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Publish the page">
    Ensure the page status is set to "Published" in greed Documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Refresh your browser or clear cache, then reload your space.
  </Step>
</Steps>
