## Overview

You can organize content in greed Documentation using spaces.

## Steps

1. Go to your greed Documentation dashboard.
2. Click "New Space" in the sidebar.
3. Enter a name and description.
4. Click "Create".

Your new space will appear in the sidebar for easy access.
