---

## Overview

Create new documentation pages easily within a space.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your space">
    Select your documentation space from the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Page'">
    Use the "Add Page" button at the top or in the navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter page details">
    Give your page a title and start writing content in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or publish">
    Click "Save Draft" or "Publish" when ready. Your page will now appear in the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>
